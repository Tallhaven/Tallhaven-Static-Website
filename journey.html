Our Journey

The story of Tallhaven began when a group of friends, passionate about software development and with firsthand experience of the dating scene, identified a glaring issue that many mainstream dating apps ignored - the significant role height plays in selecting a partner. While these apps offered a plethora of filters to help users find their ideal match, the factor of height, especially for the vertically gifted, was often overlooked, making it a challenging landscape to navigate.

Recognizing this gap, our friends set out on a journey to create Tallhaven. A unique platform that acknowledges and addresses the height factor head-on. Empowered by their tech skills and driven by a shared vision, they devoted their efforts to developing an app where height wouldn't be an afterthought but a focal point, thereby creating a comfortable space for tall individuals to explore and find their perfect match. Their journey reflects in every aspect of Tallhaven, making it more than just a dating app - a vision come to life to better serve the needs of tall singles.
